CC Certification Modules

Understand the structure of the ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity course.

Course Overview

  • 1. Security Principles: Learn the core concepts and confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA Triad).
  • 2. Business Continuity (BC) and Disaster Recovery (DR): Understand risk, incident response, and recovery planning.
  • 3. Access Control Concepts: Dive into user authentication, authorization, and identity management.
  • 4. Network Security: Get familiar with firewalls, VPNs, wireless security, and secure protocols.
  • 5. Security Operations: Explore monitoring, logging, patching, and best practices in real-world IT environments.

Each module includes interactive training, quizzes, and a final exam prep.
