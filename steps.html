How I Got My Free CC Certification

Follow these steps to earn your Certified in Cybersecurity Certificate at no cost!

  1. Create an ISC2 account: Visit the ISC2 website and sign up for a free account.
  2. Enroll in the Free CC Training: After creating your account, register for the free Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) training program.
  3. Complete the online training: Study the material at your own pace. It's self-paced and beginner-friendly.
  4. Request your free exam voucher: After completing the training, you'll receive instructions to get your exam voucher without paying.
  5. Schedule and pass the exam: Book your exam date, study carefully, and pass the CC certification exam!
  6. Celebrate! You are now Certified in Cybersecurity by ISC2!
